About FarmNive

Revolutionizing agriculture through innovation and technology

Our Mission

FarmNive (formerly AgroVision) is an AI-powered modular hydroponic farming system designed to transform sustainable agriculture. We address critical challenges including food insecurity, climate volatility, and agricultural resource scarcity.

Our solution empowers both rural and urban communities to establish reliable, sustainable food production systems that scale from home gardens to commercial operations.

Our Achievement

In April 2026, our team won first place in the graduate-level category at the Capital One/Delaware State University Innovation Venture Competition, securing $100,000 in seed funding to accelerate our mission.

"FarmNive's standout pitch—and their win—demonstrated the vital role HBCU students play in driving innovation at the intersection of technology, sustainability, and entrepreneurship."

Our Technology

Cutting-edge features for modern agriculture

Modular Hydroponics

Scalable infrastructure with automated water and nutrient management systems that adapt from home units to commercial pods.

AI-Powered Detection

Advanced computer vision and machine learning for real-time disease detection and crop health monitoring.

Digital Dashboard

User-friendly interface with mobile integration for remote monitoring and control of your farming operations.

AR/VR Training

Immersive augmented and virtual reality tools for training, monitoring, and optimizing farming practices.

Resource Optimization

Smart water and nutrient management that reduces waste and maximizes crop yield efficiency.

Data Analytics

Comprehensive analytics and insights to optimize growing conditions and improve harvest outcomes.
